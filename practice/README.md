# Practice

Welcome to the **Practice** section! This is your dedicated workspace for tackling system design challenges on your own.

## 📂 File Directory

- [`template.md`](./template.md) - A blank Markdown canvas to help you structure your own mock designs.

## 💡 Key Insights
- Passive reading is not enough. You must actively practice translating vague prompts into full system architectures.
- Practicing here builds muscle memory for your real interviews.

## 🚀 How to Use
1. Copy the [`template.md`](./template.md) file when starting a new problem.
2. Document your assumptions, draw your diagrams, and log your tradeoffs.
3. Keep your past attempts here so you can review your progress!
